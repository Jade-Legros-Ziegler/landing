What is WeBuddy?

WeBuddy is a mix of all your favorite social media to achieve one goal: learn with the help of penpals languages from all around the world. Join this awesome community to exchange with people who have the same interest as you. Enter one or more language group chats to get access to unlimited content. From posts, stories, videos, advice, and groupchats, there is a lot to discover! Immerse yourself in the wonderful world of learning with others.
